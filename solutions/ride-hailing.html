Smart Mobility

Smart Mobility

South Africa's Next Mobility Revolution — a three-pillar strategy combining EV ride-hailing, sustainable infrastructure, and integrated travel services in partnership with ACSA.

Redefining South African
Mobility

Current ride-hailing in South Africa faces critical challenges: safety concerns, unstable pricing, ongoing labour disputes, and inadequate vehicle maintenance standards. Riders deserve better, and the market is ready for transformation.

Our three-pillar strategy — developed in partnership with ACSA — brings together an EV ride-hailing platform, sustainable charging infrastructure, and an integrated online travel agency to create South Africa's first comprehensive, clean-energy mobility ecosystem.

Three Pillars

A Complete Mobility
Ecosystem

01

EV Ride-Hailing Platform

South Africa's first locally-owned electric vehicle ride-hailing service, delivering premium, safe, and sustainable transportation.

  • Direct OEM partnership with Geely Auto for custom-spec EV vehicles
  • Rigorous driver vetting and continuous safety monitoring
  • EV technology ensures lower operating costs and zero emissions
  • Premium ride experience — clean, quiet, and reliable
02

Sustainable Infrastructure

Solar-powered charging network starting at airports and expanding to commercial hubs across South Africa.

  • Solar generation + battery storage + DC fast-charging stations
  • Airport-first deployment with ACSA, scaling to malls and business parks
  • Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) and shared charging revenue model
  • Partnership with SOJO (Shenzhen-listed charging equipment manufacturer)
03

Online Travel Agency (OTA)

Building South Africa's largest OTA — connecting flights, ground transport, and accommodation in a single platform.

  • Corporate travel platform (SinoBridge) + consumer-facing booking
  • Unified data across flights, ground transport, and hotels
  • Seamless integration with the EV ride-hailing and Business Travel platforms
  • Targeting South Africa's largest OTA market position
