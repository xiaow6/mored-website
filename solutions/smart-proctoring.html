Core Capabilities

Comprehensive Assessment,
Zero Compromise

True Offline Exams

Exams run without internet or power dependency. Encrypted question papers are stored locally on devices, ensuring uninterrupted assessments even during load-shedding or connectivity failures.

AI Anti-Cheating

Six-layer detection: facial recognition, multi-person detection, abnormal behavior analysis, gaze tracking, AI-generated content detection, and similarity comparison across submissions.

Multi-Format Questions

Supports multiple choice, fill-in-the-blank, essay questions, handwritten answers, and digital scratch paper — covering all assessment formats in a single platform.

AI Question Generation

Automatically generates multi-level questions based on Bloom's Taxonomy, enabling lecturers to quickly create assessments that test knowledge, comprehension, analysis, and synthesis.

AI Auto-Grading

AI-powered automated grading for objective and subjective answers, with a built-in human review workflow allowing lecturers to verify, adjust, and approve marks before publication.

Dual Camera Monitoring

Front-facing camera plus a secondary side camera provide comprehensive visual coverage. AI automatically clips suspicious segments for efficient lecturer review.

How It Works

From Setup to Results

01

Setup

Create exam modules and configure parameters — open/closed book, camera requirements, time limits, and question types.

02

Verify

Environment detection and facial recognition match candidates against registered enrollment data before the exam begins.

03

Exam

Students answer offline on tablets, PCs, or mobile devices while the system provides real-time AI monitoring and proctoring.

04

Review

AI flags suspicious clips for lecturer review. Auto-grading generates marks, lecturers verify and approve, then results are published.
