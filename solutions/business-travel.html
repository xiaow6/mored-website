End-to-End Travel
Management

Streamlined Approval Workflow

Multi-level approval chains with real-time notifications via WhatsApp, email, and mobile push. Managers can approve travel requests on the go, eliminating bottlenecks.

Intuitive Online Booking

One-stop search for flights and hotels with direct GDS connections to Amadeus, Sabre, and Travelport. Compare prices, apply policies, and book in minutes — no agents needed.

Secure Digital Payments

Integrated with university financial systems for seamless payment processing. Automated reconciliation ensures every transaction is tracked, verified, and audit-ready.

Financial Management

Real-time budget monitoring, department-level spend analysis, and comprehensive audit trails. Finance teams get full visibility into travel expenditure across the organization.

AI customer service chatbot for instant support, AI OCR for receipt scanning and expense claims, and intelligent destination recommendations based on travel patterns.

HR system integration, local payment methods, and South African compliance workflows. Built specifically for the regulatory and operational requirements of SA enterprises.
